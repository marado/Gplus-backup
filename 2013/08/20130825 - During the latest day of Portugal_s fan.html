During the latest day of Portugal's fantastic Entremuralhas festival for 2013, as an warmup for the afternoon concerts at Palco Pena, the Portuguese post-cyberpunk act "Kokori" is going to Release some Candid Hate.
At the lounge of Eurosol, in the city's center, the duo is finally going to present their latest work, "Release Candid Hate", there being officially released in both their formats: a (regular edition) 7" EP, or a (special, limited edition) 7"+5" EP (both releases also offering a free download version of its music).
Release Candid Hate marks a new step for Kokori, with harsh lyrics and marked voices to add to the post-cyberpunk ambience you might know from their previous works.
And because they want to spread the code, every order during the event will get you a free beer, with compliments from the band.

See you there!
