Originally shared by Jan WildeboerRemember how I said right from the start of #DieselGate that instead of only blaming VW it would be far better to take a look at how the ECU (Engine Control Unit) they use, which is from Bosch, works? Felix Domke did just that at #32c3 in this talk. https://events.ccc.de/congress/2015/Fahrplan/events/7331.html

It's exactly as I suspected. Bosch controls the code and keeps it proprietary. VW and other car manufacturers that use their stuff (and that's a lot of car manufacturers. Bosch is quite close to a monopoly in this market) can only influence some very narrow parameters. So I ask again: how much blame is at Bosch?
