"The first handsets with Cyanogen OS are launched on the market in Spain starting today, and will arrive to Germany and the United Kingdom in the coming weeks. Throughout 2016, Telefónica will bring these devices to other countries in which it operates.

The first will be Aquaris X5, which will be available in the Movistar commercial network in Spain from November 11 for 209 euros (VAT included). Movistar customers can finance it up to 24 months interest-free for a subscription fee costing as little as 8.7 Euro."
